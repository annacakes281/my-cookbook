How to make your own pizza sauce

In this section you will learn how to make your own home-made pizza sauce, which can be used for the pizza.

Follow along with this easy-to-make pizza sauce, which can also be used with other recipes such as pastas and curries!

Happy cooking! 🍽


You can tick off each step once you have completed it


1.Pizza Sauce Equipment

  • Medium Saucepan
  • Chopping board
  • Knife
  • Wooden spoon

2.Pizza Sauce Ingredients

  • 500g pot or box of passata
  • Salt and pepper to taste - I used a teaspoon of each in mine
  • 3 chopped garlic cloves - again to taste
  • Dried herbs (1/2 tablespoon) - I used thyme and oregano, 1/2 tablespoon of each

3.Pizza Sauce Steps

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cooking Time: 10-15 minutes

Cooldown Time: 30-45 minutes


  1. Pour the passata into a saucepan and bring to a simmer over a medium heat
  2. While the passata is being brought to a simmer, chop the garlic into small pieces
  3. Once the passata is simmering, add the garlic, salt and pepper, and the dried herbs, mix for 5 minutes on a low-medium heat, ensuring that it is still simmering.
    Taste and add more salt and pepper if necessary remember it will be hot so be careful
  4. Once ready, take off heat and let it cool down completely before using the sauce for the pizza
